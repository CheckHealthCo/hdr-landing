About us

Referring patients for expert care amongst doctors is now, easy and informal.

HelloDr is a digital platform presented exclusively for doctors to refer patients amongst their professional community. By eliminating paper, pencil & calls, it saves your precious time.

This app helps you to share & discuss with your colleagues, the clinical aspects of diagnosis and management protocols, in the best interest of your patients without breach of privacy.

Irrespective of time and places where your practices are situated, the uniqueness of the app allows you to keep track of the patients referrals over a period of time.

HelloDr allows your fellow doctors to be aware of your key expertise in the chosen field of your passion. Similarly, it helps doctors unknown to each other to connect & refer patients requiring expert care.
