Communicate with ease

Use today's technology to deliver your message.

Clear communication lays a solid foundation for a successful referral. HelloDr makes it easy and dead simple to send a referral. And each referral is seggregated from the other. No more confusions or mix-ups.

HelloDr ensures delivery of each message by sms. So even if the doctor you refer to does not have the app, the concerned doctor will be notified about the referral. Having the app though, makes it much easier to communicate regarding the patient. You can exchange images and videos as well in addition to messages if you have the app.

Realtime Messaging service

Get the information across quickly.

HelloDr messaging service is in real time no matter at which corner of the earth you are located. Leave a message for your doctor to see. Or better still, if available, start a one to one chat with him. Leave a thank you note or even send an invitation for a talk. Its a private channel to your favorite doctor

The more information you provide, the easier it is for your doctor to get to a diagnosis quickly. Upload the xray or the ultrasound report or even clinical pictures, and HelloDr will deliver it to your doctor. Or maybe you want to upload short videos demoing a particular clinical feature. Its up to you. And unlimited messaging once you register.

Privacy

All the information you exchange stays secure and private.

All messages stay within the app. No one has access to the information exchange. Messages are posted over secure channels and stay safe from snooping eyes. No more posting in public forums.

Your contact info stays locked and hidden by default until you decide to display it. Period.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

You can send unlimited referrals. There is absolutely no charge for it. For receving referrals, we offer a month's trial plan. After a month, you will need to buy a subscription plan if you want to receive unrestricted referrals through the app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all the major credit cards, pay wallets, UPI and most bank accounts for net banking.

Can I change my plan later?

You are free to change the plan anytime you want. Prorata charges will apply if you drop out in the middle of a month you have already subscribed to.

Do you have a contract?

We do not believe in long time contracts. You are free to subscribe or unsubscribe any time. :)
